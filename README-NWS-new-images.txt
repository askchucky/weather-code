On July 7, 2015, the US National Weather Service implemented a new set of icons for
weather conditions reporting, and a new 6-hour icon dynamically created when there are
changing conditions during the 12 hour forecast.  To do this, a new script (DualImage.php)
was created to blend two images together from image templates (in forecast/icon-templates)
and a resulting image displayed to the browser.

An updated advforecast2.php V4.00 (6-Jul-2015) was released to support the dual-image 
function.
